Crontab syntax is one of those things I re-learn from scratch every time I need it. MacCron is a small Python GUI for adding, editing, and removing cron jobs on macOS without opening `crontab -e` and squinting at asterisks.

See the [source on GitHub](https://github.com/kyletaylored/maccron) for installation instructions.
