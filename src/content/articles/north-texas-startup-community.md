[Startup Weekend Denton](http://denton.startupweekend.org)

A local event aimed at supporting entrepreneurs and the startup community. Pitch an idea, build a team, and develop a product in 54-hours with the end goal of launching a startup at the end of the weekend.

[Startup Digest Dallas](http://startupdigest.com/dallas)

A weekly publication that highlights tech and startup events in the North Texas community.

[BigDOCC](https://www.facebook.com/BigDOCC) (BigD Open Coffee Club)

BigDOCC is a gathering of technology enthusiasts, startups, and those that practice a digital lifestyle. For one hour each week, we discuss the latest news, over coffee. We’re interested in starting a LittleDOCC in Denton for the community.
