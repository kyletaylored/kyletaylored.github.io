At FFW I worked client-facing across both sides of the fence — supporting sales and delivery teams on new engagements through scoping, estimating, and proposal writing, and consulting on active projects as a general technologist. That meant discovery and architecture documentation for Drupal and WordPress builds, site and performance audits, DevOps/CI tooling, and reporting recommendations, depending on what a given client actually needed.

It was the first job where the work was explicitly about bridging technical and non-technical teams rather than just building — a direction that carried straight into the sales engineering roles that followed.
