I joined Datadog in May 2024 as a Senior Sales Engineer, coming over from a Principal Sales Engineer role at Pantheon. My background in web application development and cloud infrastructure let me ramp quickly — instead of easing in, I jumped straight into building packages and enablement tooling, and became one of the first Real User Monitoring (RUM) subject matter experts at Datadog.

Most of that work shows up as small, public tools rather than one big project: a request/response interceptor for debugging RUM sessions, an educational sandbox for how RUM tracks fragments and actions, and Datadog Mobile SDK support for .NET MAUI apps (including the symbol-upload tooling needed to make MAUI stack traces readable). I've also been experimenting with agentic AI on Azure and Datadog as that space has opened up.
