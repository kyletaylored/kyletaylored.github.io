Composition Rules

How to use these without making a mess.

01
Go large or go home
Shapes should be 100–200px minimum. Small shapes read as mistakes. If a circle isn't making an impact, double its size.
02
Clip at edges
Position circles and triangles so they bleed off the edge of the section. Fully contained shapes look like clip art. Partially hidden = intentional composition.
03
Triangle behind, circle in front
The teal triangle always sits behind the pink circle (lower z-index). The circle overlaps the triangle. This is the canonical pairing.
04
Right side, always
Art compositions live on the right side of dark hero sections. Left side is reserved for text. The terminal window sits between text and art.
05
Maximum 3 accent colors per scene
Pink + teal + yellow is the canonical hero trio. Don't mix all 6 palette colors in one composition — pick 3.
06
Small details anchor the large shapes
Always pair large shapes with micro-details: a crosshair, slash marks + yellow square, a dot grid, a diamond. These create the "blueprint" feel that makes the composition feel engineered, not random.
