Usage Rules

K
✓ DO: Teal on any bg
The primary mark always uses #28D7E5 fill with #111 K.
K
✕ DON'T: Flip or rotate
The K has directional weight. Never mirror, rotate, or skew.
K
✕ DON'T: Gradient fill
Solid #28D7E5 only. No gradients, no patterns inside the mark.
K
✕ DON'T: Reduce opacity
Always full opacity. Use the outline variant for ghosted contexts instead.
